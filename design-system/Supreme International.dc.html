01 — Design Strategy

Corporate gifting,
engineered like software.

Supreme International turns a manual sales process — discovery, quotation, follow-up — into a self-serve platform. The visual language pairs the trust of an enterprise SaaS dashboard with the warmth of a luxury gifting brand: deep indigo, living teal, frosted glass over soft light.

01
Self-serve quotations
Browse, filter, shortlist to cart, generate a priced quotation PDF — delivered by WhatsApp, email or download.
02
Instant catalogues
Apply filters, compile every matching product into one branded PDF — pricing intentionally excluded, ready to circulate.
03
Merchandise portals
Per-company branded stores where employees spend allocated points or coupons and cover any overage themselves.
